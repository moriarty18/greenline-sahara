## Idea 1: Eco-Cyberpunk (Selected)

**Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / High-Tech Eco
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Neon & Darkness**: Utilize the strong contrast between the dark environment and the vibrant green neon lights from the station photos.
2.  **Speed & Energy**: Visuals should convey fast charging and high power (120kW).
3.  **Mobile-First Utility**: Since the user comes from WhatsApp, the UI must be instantly understandable and actionable on mobile.
4.  **Bilingual Fluidity**: Seamless switching between English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL) without breaking the layout.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: Deep charcoal/black (`#0a0a0a`, `#121212`) to match the underground parking vibe and make the green pop.
*   **Primary**: Electric Green (`#00ff00` or similar from the photos) for CTAs and accents.
*   **Text**: White for headings, light gray for body text to ensure readability on dark backgrounds.
*   **Intent**: To evoke a sense of modern, cutting-edge technology and premium service.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Vertical Flow**: A strong vertical scroll tailored for mobile devices.
*   **Card-Based Sections**: Information (Location, Features, Apps) grouped in semi-transparent dark cards with subtle neon borders.
*   **Immersive Hero**: Full-screen hero image with the station glowing, immediate CTA.

**Signature Elements**:
*   **Neon Glow Effects**: CSS box-shadows and text-shadows mimicking the charging station lights.
*   **Tech Patterns**: Subtle circuit board or grid overlays in the background.
*   **Floating Action Button (FAB)**: For "Get Directions" or "Download App" that sticks to the bottom on mobile.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Instant Feedback**: Buttons glow brighter on touch/hover.
*   **Smooth Scroll**: Navigation links scroll smoothly to sections.

**Animation**:
*   **Pulse**: The "120 kW" and "Available Now" badges pulse gently.
*   **Slide-in**: Elements slide in from the bottom as the user scrolls.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: 'Orbitron' or 'Rajdhani' (Tech/Futuristic sans-serif) for English; 'Tajawal' or 'Cairo' for Arabic (modern, geometric).
*   **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Roboto' for high readability; 'Almarai' for Arabic body text.

## Idea 2: Clean Green Energy

**Design Movement**: Minimalist Eco-Modernism
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Clarity & Freshness**: Focus on the "Green" in Greenline. Bright, airy, and clean.
2.  **Accessibility**: High contrast, large touch targets, very easy to read in bright sunlight (e.g., if user is outside).
3.  **Trust & Reliability**: Professional look that emphasizes safety and established infrastructure.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: White or very light gray (`#f5f5f5`).
*   **Primary**: Forest Green (`#2e7d32`) combined with the bright brand green.
*   **Text**: Dark slate (`#1e293b`).
*   **Intent**: To feel friendly, approachable, and environmentally conscious.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Clean Grid**: Standard, reliable layout.
*   **Split Screen**: Text on one side, image on the other (stacking on mobile).

**Signature Elements**:
*   **Leaf/Nature Motifs**: Subtle background patterns.
*   **Rounded Corners**: Soft, friendly UI elements.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Standard & Predictable**: No surprises, standard button behaviors.

**Animation**:
*   **Fade-in**: Gentle fades.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: 'Montserrat' (Geometric sans).
*   **Body**: 'Open Sans'.

## Idea 3: Premium Automotive

**Design Movement**: Luxury Automotive UI
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Exclusivity**: Treat the charging station like a luxury car showroom.
2.  **Performance**: Emphasize the specs (120kW) like car specs.
3.  **Sleekness**: Metallic gradients, sharp lines.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: Dark Gunmetal (`#1c1c1c`) with metallic gradients.
*   **Primary**: Silver and Neon Green.
*   **Intent**: To appeal to owners of high-end EVs (Tesla, Porsche, etc.).

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Magazine Style**: Large typography, overlapping images.

**Signature Elements**:
*   **Metallic Textures**: Gradients on buttons and backgrounds.
*   **Speed Lines**: Italics and slanted shapes.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Snappy**: Fast transitions.

**Animation**:
*   **Parallax**: Background moves slower than foreground.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: 'Eurostile' (Wide, speed-focused).
*   **Body**: 'Lato'.
